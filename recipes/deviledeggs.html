Deviled Eggs

Description

Deviled eggs are hard boiled eggs where the yolk is mixed with mayonnaise, mustard, salt and pepper. The little sprinkle of paprika on top is the perfect finishing touch.

Ingredients

  • 6 Eggs
  • 1/4 cup Mayonnaise
  • 2 tsp Yellow Mustard
  • 1/8 tsp Salt
  • Ground Pepper
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Hard boil eggs in Instapot
  2. After pressure has been released, place eggs in bowl of ice water for 4 minutes
  3. Rinse, then carefully crack and peel eggs
  4. Slice eggs in half
  5. Remove egg yolks with a spoon and place in mixing bowl
  6. Mash up egg yolks into a fine crumble, then add mayo, mustard, salt, and pepper
  7. Evenly dollop mixture back in to center of egg whites
  8. Sprinkle with paprika
