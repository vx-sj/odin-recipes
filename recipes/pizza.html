Pizza

Description

A classic italian dish made with flattened bread dough and spread with a savory mixture of tomatoes cheese and pepperoni and baked in the oven.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 20 slices of pepperoni
  • 12 oz mozzarella cheese
  • 1 teaspoon fresh oregano
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 500 degrees
  2. Roll out the dough into 14 in circle
  3. Spread sauce out across dough
  4. Cover with cheese
  5. Place pepperonis evenly across
  6. Cook for 10 min
  7. Eat and Enjoy!
